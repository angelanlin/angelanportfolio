About Me

My name is Angelan or Angie, as my friends refer to me. I am an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign majoring in BFA Graphic Design. Creating art is a way to heal myself by exploring human stories and emotions told through different mediums. I am a designer with a multitude of passions in various mediums who seeks to create art that inspires others!
